My Baseball Career

I have been playing baseball ever since I could swing a wiffle-ball bat, but I really started to get serious about baseball the summer after my sophomore year in high school. My Junior year I started at second base and was elected captain my senior year. Last year, I played second base for the Norse. While we finished at the bottom of the conference last year, we have a new coach this year and are hungry. Don't sleep on Norse baseball...

My Favorite Team

This should go without saying, but my favorite team is the Minnesota Twins. Although we have been sub-par for the last few years, we have some young talent in our farm system. If Buxton and Sano can stay healthy, they will be major contributors to the MLB roster, joining the promising rookie Vargas. While the present is dismal, the future is bright for Twins baseball.
